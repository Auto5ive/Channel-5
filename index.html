FAQ

What is Channel 5.0? +
Channel 5.0 is a 555-piece cyberpunk NFT collection on Ethereum. Holding one gives you lifetime access to Auto 5ive — a Discord bot that automates NFT minting, marketplace trading, wallet tracking and phase detection across 17+ EVM chains.
What is Auto 5ive and what can it do? +
Auto 5ive is an NFT operations bot running on Discord. It can: mint NFTs across 17+ chains using multiple wallets simultaneously, check whitelist eligibility, buy/sell/sweep on OpenSea via Seaport v1.6, track any wallet for NFT activity, detect phase changes and alert you in real time, and much more — all from Discord commands.
How do I get access to Auto 5ive? +
Hold a Channel 5.0 NFT. Once verified, you get a private session channel in the Discord server with full bot access. No monthly fee, no subscription — your NFT is your lifetime key.
How do I earn a guaranteed whitelist spot? +
Enter the referral program via the Dashboard page. Connect your wallet, complete social tasks (follow, repost, tag friends), and share your unique invite link. The top referrers on the leaderboard earn guaranteed mint spots. Top 10–20 spots are secured before public mint opens.
Is my wallet / private key safe with Auto 5ive? +
Private keys are encrypted at rest and only decrypted in memory at the moment of a transaction. There is a rate limiter on key operations — max 30 decrypts per minute per user. Keys are added via DM with the bot so they are never visible in public channels. The database is not stored in plaintext.
How many wallets can I add to Auto 5ive? +
Each user can add up to 10 wallets. Bot admins can grant wallet limit overrides for power users. All wallets are accessible via commands like !mint W1,W2,W3 or !mint all.
What chains does Auto 5ive support? +
Ethereum, Base, Optimism, Polygon, Arbitrum, zkSync, Abstract, Linea, Blast, Avalanche, Sonic, Berachain, ApeChain, HyperLiquid EVM, MegaETH, Monad, Celo, and more. New chains are added regularly.
What is the mint price and when does it drop? +
Supply is 555. Mint price depends on your phase — community collaborators mint free, while beta testers and the public phase are paid. Whitelist spots via the referral program are secured first. The exact date and public price will be announced — whitelist spots will be revealed first. Join the leaderboard campaign now to secure your free spot.
Can Auto 5ive mint any OpenSea drop or just specific ones? +
Auto 5ive supports any OpenSea SeaDrop contract (ERC721SeaDropV1, ERC721SeaDropV2, ERC1155) across all supported chains. Just pass the contract address or OpenSea slug and the bot handles the rest — phase detection, eligibility, and execution.
Does the bot work 24/7 even when I'm offline? +
Yes. Auto 5ive runs on a dedicated server (managed via PM2) that stays online 24/7. You can schedule mints, set phase watchers, and set up floor/offer alerts — the bot will execute and post results to your private channel while you sleep.
